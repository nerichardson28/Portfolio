Feild Trips




Theory

Field trips expose kids to hands on learning experiences instead of sitting in the classroom learning from a book.

Benefits/Positives

- Hands on Learning
- Makes learning relevant through real word connections
- Exposes students to new cultures, places and ideas

Drawbacks/Challeneges

-Planning is time consuming
-Can be expensive
-Safety Concerns
- keeping kids well behaved can be difficult in a large group
