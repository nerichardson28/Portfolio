Instructional Methods Portfolio

Demonstrations




Theory

To make abstract ideas seem more concrete and help visual learners in the learning process by showing processes step by step. This then will increase motivation, understanding and motivation.

Benefits/Positives

-Strengthens Understanding
-helpful in remembering material
-Attention Grabbing

Drawbacks/Challeneges

-potential limited student interaction
-limited resources
-limited participation

Grade/Unit/Outcome

Grade:10-12
Subject:Psychology
Outcome: On my placement I had the students act out the ID, EGO & Super EGO and show how they work together according to Sigmund Freud

Summary

Demonstrations can be a great learning tool, especially if there are alot of visual learners in the classroom.It promotes engagement, participation and retention of infomation
