Book Tasting




Theory

Booking tasting is an engaging way of exposing kids to genres, authors and titles of books that they may not have considered.And could be potentially help foster a new interest in reading

Benefits/Positives

-Provides insight into students interests
-Promotes critical thinking
-Engages apathetic readers

Drawbacks/Challeneges

- Potential for disinterest
-Planning is Time Consuming
-Classroom Managment challenges
-may be difficult to offer an abudant supply of diverse books

Grade/Subject/Outcome

Grade:9-12
Subject:ELA
Outcomw:to get ideas for a novel study that the students would be interested in

Summary

Book tastings are a good way to expose kids to different kinds of books, pique their interest and help teachers take the books they're interested in and incoproate them into classroom lessons ex.novel study
